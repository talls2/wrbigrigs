What We Do

Our Services

From custom fleet paint to major collision repair, we handle every job with the same attention to detail — no matter the size of the rig.

🎨 Custom Painting

Full custom paint jobs for semi-trucks, tankers, and big rigs. Single colors, fleet graphics, or full custom designs.

🔧 Collision Repair

Certified technicians restore your truck to factory condition. Frame straightening, panel replacement, full structural repair.

🏗 Heavy Equipment

We paint loaders, excavators, cranes, and heavy machinery. Any size, any color, any brand.

🚚 Fleet Services

Keep your entire fleet looking sharp and road-ready. Volume pricing available for fleet operators and owner-operators.

⚙ Body Work & Fabrication

Dent removal, metal fabrication, cab and chassis body work. We fix the damage others turn away.

🛡 Insurance Claims

We work directly with all major carriers to streamline your claim and get your truck back on the road faster.
